Our services

Our Services focus on Cold Rolling Mills and Process line Machinery, below are services offered can be scaled to individual project requirements.

  • Factory Planning for equipment production, material flow and Quality Control.
  • Equipment Foundation Design, Detailed design from excavation plans to Embedded Steel.
  • Outsourcing for Industrial Machinery manufacturing, USA and World sources.
  • Machine Assembly, Test and Quality Control Reporting.
  • Machine installation Supervision and labor as required.
